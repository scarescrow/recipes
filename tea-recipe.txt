Ingredients:
 - 1 spoon sugar
 - 1 teaspoon tea leaves
 - 1 1/2 cup water
 - 1 1/2 teaspoons milk
 
Directions:
 1. Pour the water into  teapot and heat it till it boils.
 
 2. Once water starts boiling, switch off the flame, and add the tea leaves. Keep
    the leaves immersed for one minute
 
 3. Once color is obtained, pour the tea into a cup. Add the milk and sugar. Stir
    properly.
